REWRITE Hello and welcome to my site!

My name is Tyler Sherman and I am a current sophomore in the College of Engineering at Cornell University. I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) and a minor in Business, with an anticipated graduation date of May 2020.

My passion for engineering came about rather unexpectedly. In 2004, the day before my father was leaving for a long work trip, he bought me a 2,000-piece K’Nex rollercoaster set. I had never owned or worked on a kit like this before, but once I started, I couldn’t tear myself away. To my family’s amazement, I finished it before my dad left for his flight the next morning. From the first rod-and-connector pair, a fire burned inside of me that had lain dormant. This was the first of many K’Nex projects I would build over the next eight years. Working with my hands and building, seeing a complex structure come to life, was deeply satisfying. My brain filled with thoughts of “Why?” and “How?” questions that to this day continue to drive my actions, both inside and outside the classroom.

I am originally from Chappaqua, NY, although I was fortunate enough to spend two of my elementary school years in Melbourne, Australia. I grew up swimming competitively and playing travel soccer; I absolutely love all water sports and spending time outdoors.

In my time outside of the classroom, I am a brother of the Beta Theta Chapter of Pi Kappa Alpha, work as one of only five Team Leaders in the Marriott Student Learning Center in the School of Hotel Administration, serve as an Executive Board member of Habitat for Humanity, am a member of the Guiding Eyes for the Blind organization, and volunteer as an Orientation Leader.

I am a self-disciplined, motivated, and passionate individual who enjoys challenging himself and working with others. I look forward to expanding my capabilities and am eager to gain experience in the engineering field, specifically in the sectors of Electrical Engineering, Software Engineering, and Computer Hardware. Kindly connect with me on LinkedIn or fill out the contact form below with any questions or opportunities.
